The Voyage

Overfloaters is an instrumental nujazz/fusion trio from Prague, weaving musical narratives without words. Drawing inspiration from cinematic worlds, folklore, and vivid storytelling, their compositions blend rich textures, evolving melodies, and genre-bending improvisation.

Each track unfolds like a journey — from the mystical medieval kingdom of Sedmý Král (The Seventh King) to the open seas of their debut Мореход (Sailor).

With roots in jazz, fusion, and experimental sounds, Overfloaters create immersive soundscapes that invite the listener to imagine, drift, and return — transformed.

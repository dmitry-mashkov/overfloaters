The Voyage

Overfloaters is an instrumental nujazz/fusion band from Prague, weaving musical narratives without words. Drawing inspiration from cinematic worlds, folklore, and vivid storytelling, their compositions blend rich textures, evolving melodies, and genre-bending improvisation.

Each track unfolds like a journey — from the mystical medieval kingdom of Sedmý Král (The Seventh King) to the open seas of their debut Мореход (Sailor).
